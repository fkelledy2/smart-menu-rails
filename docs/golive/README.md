## What Is mellow.menu?

mellow.menu is a multi-tenant restaurant SaaS platform that replaces the paper menu, the printed QR code, and the back-of-napkin pricing exercise with a single intelligent system. Restaurants get a digital menu with 40+ language localisation, AI-assisted pricing and bundling, QR-code table ordering, bill-splitting, OCR menu import, AI-generated food photography, and real-time order and kitchen management — all under one subscription.

The platform is production-ready. The DNS is live. The go-to-market work begins now.

---

## Strategic Priorities for Launch

The single most important goal for the first 90 days is to **get 10 paying restaurants live on the platform** and turn their real-world usage into case studies, testimonials, and referral leads.

Everything in this plan is sequenced toward that goal. Paid advertising, PR, and content marketing are secondary until social proof exists.

## Platform Capabilities Summary (for marketing reference)

- Digital menus with 40+ language localisation via DeepL
- AI menu optimisation (pricing recommendations, bundling, menu engineering)
- QR code table ordering
- Online ordering with bill-splitting (Ordrparticipants)
- OCR menu import from photos and PDFs (Google Cloud Vision)
- AI-generated food photography (DALL-E)
- Multi-payment provider support (Stripe + Square)
- Real-time order and kitchen management
- Profit margin tracking and cost analytics
- Employee role management
- Menu versioning and A/B experiments
- Floorplan management
- Alcohol policy enforcement

## Critical Path to First 10 Paying Restaurants

1. Complete pre-launch checklist (02) — estimated 2 weeks
2. Identify and personally onboard 5 anchor restaurants (soft launch) — weeks 3–6
3. Capture testimonials and one mini case study — weeks 5–8
4. Execute public launch (Product Hunt, LinkedIn, press) — week 8
5. Begin outbound sales sequence to 50 qualified prospects — week 8 onward

## Key Dependencies to Resolve Before Launch

These are flagged in full in [06_tech_roadmap_inputs.md](06_tech_roadmap_inputs.md):

1. Public menu pages need SEO-optimised structured data (schema.org/Menu)
2. A referral/affiliate tracking system is needed before launching a referral programme
3. The demo booking flow (form + Calendly integration) must be live before any paid traffic
4. A free trial tier with usage-gated feature flags is required for PLG motion
5. A public-facing pricing page with live plan data must be built
