# mellow.menu Blog Strategy & Implementation

## Overview
Develop a comprehensive blog strategy to establish mellow.menu as a thought leader in restaurant technology, drive organic traffic, generate qualified leads, and support overall marketing objectives.

## Business Objectives
- **Thought Leadership**: Establish mellow.menu as an authority in restaurant technology
- **Lead Generation**: Drive qualified restaurant owner and manager leads
- **SEO Enhancement**: Improve search rankings for target keywords
- **Brand Awareness**: Increase brand visibility and recognition
- **Community Building**: Create a community of restaurant technology enthusiasts

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Restaurant Owners**: Decision-makers for technology adoption
- **Restaurant Managers**: Operations-focused leaders
- **Beverage Directors**: Wine and beverage program managers
- **IT Directors**: Technology decision-makers in hospitality

### Secondary Audience
- **Sommeliers**: Wine professionals seeking technology solutions
- **Chefs**: Culinary professionals interested in innovation
- **Hospitality Students**: Future industry leaders
- **Technology Investors**: Potential investors and partners

### Tertiary Audience
- **Food Bloggers**: Influencers in dining and technology
- **Industry Journalists**: Media covering restaurant technology
- **Consultants**: Hospitality and technology consultants
- **Event Planners**: Corporate and private event organizers

## Editorial Calendar

### Publishing Schedule
- **Frequency**: 3-4 posts per week
- **Consistency**: Regular publishing schedule
- **Quality over Quantity**: Focus on high-value content
- **Seasonal Relevance**: Align with restaurant industry cycles

### Monthly Themes
- **January**: New Year, New Technology - Planning and implementation

## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Strategy
- **Primary Keywords**: Restaurant technology, digital menus, AI sommelier
- **Secondary Keywords**: Wine pairing software, restaurant innovation, dining technology
- **Long-tail Keywords**: How to implement restaurant technology, AI in fine dining
- **Local Keywords**: Restaurant technology [city], digital menus [region]

### On-Page SEO
- **Title Optimization**: Compelling, keyword-rich titles
- **Meta Descriptions**: Engaging descriptions with clear CTAs
- **Header Structure**: Proper H1, H2, H3 hierarchy
- **Internal Linking**: Strategic linking to relevant content
- **Image Optimization**: Alt tags and file names

### Content Optimization
- **Readability**: Clear, accessible language
- **Length**: Comprehensive, in-depth content (1,500-2,500 words)
- **Multimedia**: Images, videos, infographics
- **Schema Markup**: Article, FAQ, and HowTo schema
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design and fast loading
