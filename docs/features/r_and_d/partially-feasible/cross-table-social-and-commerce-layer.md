# Cross-Table Social & Commerce Layer

**Status:** Research / Strategy
**Feasibility:** Partially Feasible / Constrained by Current Platforms
**Target:** 2026+

## Vision

Turn the dining room into a network where tables can interact, gift, vote, and participate in live restaurant moments.

## Included Ideas

- Send drinks to another table
- Live dining polls
- Cross-table prompts and participatory moments

## Feasible Now

- Server-mediated gifting between tables
- Live polls and restaurant-wide participation moments
- Table-targeted payments with staff approval workflows

## Constraints

- Requires strong abuse prevention
- Table addressing must be trustworthy
- Social features must fit venue tone and brand

## Suggested R&D Path

- Start with opt-in experiences in bars, events, and hospitality-led venues
- Require staff approval for inter-table gifting
- Use this as a social layer, not a core ordering dependency

## Strategic Value

- Makes the venue feel alive and participatory
- Opens new hospitality and revenue mechanics
- Best suited to restaurants with social energy, events, or entertainment formats
