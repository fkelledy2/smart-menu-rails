## Phase 1 — Soft Launch / Beta
**Duration:** April 22 – May 19, 2026 (4 weeks)
**Owner:** Founder

### Objective
Get 5–10 restaurants actively using the platform. Learn what breaks. Gather testimonials and real-world usage data. Fix onboarding friction before exposing the product to strangers.

### Why Soft Launch First?
- Founder-led onboarding of known restaurants catches product bugs before they become public reviews
- Personal relationships mean honest, direct feedback
- Testimonials from real restaurants are worth more than any paid ad at this stage
- Every friction point in onboarding costs 5x more to fix after it's been shown to thousands of people

### Target Restaurant Profiles for Phase 1
Prioritise restaurants that match the Tier 1 ICP:
- Tourist-facing or multicultural urban location (multilingual feature is immediately valuable)
- Owner-operated or small team (fast decision-making)
- Currently using paper or static QR menus (clear upgrade path)
- Willing to give feedback and be a reference account

### Pilot Offer (Phase 1 Only)
- **3 months free** on the Professional plan in exchange for:
  - Attending a 30-minute onboarding call
  - Providing written feedback after Week 2
  - Allowing use of their name/logo (with approval) in marketing materials
  - Optionally providing a written testimonial

## Phase 2 — Public Launch
**Date:** Week of May 20, 2026
**Owner:** Founder (full focus week — clear calendar)

### Objective
Generate awareness, inbound leads, and first self-serve signups. Goal: 20+ demo bookings in the launch week; 5+ self-serve trial signups.

### Launch Week Plan

#### Monday, May 20 — Soft Online Warm-Up
- Email your personal network (investors, advisors, friends, former colleagues): "mellow.menu is live — would love your support on Product Hunt this Thursday"
- Post a "We're launching this week" teaser on LinkedIn (personal profile, not company page)
- DM 20 people personally and ask them to share the Product Hunt launch

#### Tuesday, May 21 — Content Push
- Publish the first blog post / case study from a pilot restaurant
- Share it on LinkedIn with the story of how the restaurant used mellow.menu
- Post a short Loom video demo clip on LinkedIn (60 seconds, "how mellow.menu works")

#### Wednesday, May 22 — Press and Outreach
- Send press release to 5–10 hospitality trade publications (see channel strategy for targets)
- Email 30 restaurant owner prospects directly (personalised, short, with the case study link)
- Post in 3–5 relevant Facebook groups and Reddit/hospitality forums

#### Thursday, May 23 — Product Hunt Launch Day
- Launch on Product Hunt at 12:01 AM Pacific (08:01 AM Irish / 09:01 AM CET)
- Post the Product Hunt link on all channels: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, email your network again
- Respond to every single Product Hunt comment personally within 30 minutes
- Ask all pilot restaurant owners to leave a review on Product Hunt (give them the link the night before)
- Founder to be available 8 AM–10 PM to respond to inbound

#### Friday, May 24 — Follow Up and Convert
- Send a "we launched and here's what we learned" LinkedIn post
- Follow up personally with everyone who commented or upvoted on Product Hunt
- Book demo calls with all inbound leads from the week
- Send a thank-you email to everyone who shared or supported the launch

### Launch Week Channels
| Channel | Action | Owner | Expected Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product Hunt | Full launch with assets | Founder | 200–500 upvotes; 5–15 trial signups |
| LinkedIn (personal) | Daily posts all week | Founder | 2,000–5,000 impressions; 10–20 leads |
| Personal email network | Launch email to 500+ | Founder | 50–100 opens; 5–10 referrals |
| Hospitality press | Press release | Founder | 1–3 coverage mentions |
| Restaurant Facebook groups | Manual posts | Founder | 5–15 direct conversations |
| Outbound email (30 targets) | Personalised cold email | Founder | 2–5 replies; 1–2 demo bookings |

### Launch Week Assets Required
- [ ] Product Hunt page (tagline, description, 5 screenshots, demo GIF, maker comment)
- [ ] Launch email for personal network
- [ ] LinkedIn launch announcement post (with video)
- [ ] Press release (300 words, factual, with quote from pilot restaurant owner)
- [ ] Blog post / case study from pilot restaurant
- [ ] 5 screenshots of the product (clean, branded)
- [ ] 60-second demo video or GIF
