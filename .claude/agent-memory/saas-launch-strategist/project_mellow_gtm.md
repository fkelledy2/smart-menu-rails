## Key Positioning Decisions

Primary positioning: "All-in-one digital menu and ordering platform that helps independent and growing restaurants serve international guests, optimise their most profitable items, and run smoother table service — without needing a developer or a separate system for every problem."

Three differentiating pillars:
1. Multilingual by default (40+ languages via DeepL) — no direct competitor owns this
2. AI-powered revenue intelligence (pricing, bundling, margin tracking)
3. End-to-end table experience (QR order, bill-splitting, payments)

## ICP Priority Order

Tier 1 (target first): Independent restaurant owner, tourist district or urban multicultural market, 30–150 covers, 1 location, already on Square or Stripe, currently using paper or static QR menus. Cities: London, Dublin, Amsterdam, Lisbon, Sydney, NYC, Melbourne.

Tier 2 (after traction): Multi-location casual chain, 2–10 sites, operations manager or marketing head as buyer.

Tier 3 (do not target at launch): Enterprise, hotel F&B, franchises.
